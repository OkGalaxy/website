What is Osu Standard?

Osu!Standard or better known as just osu is a rhythm game revolved around clicking circles to a beat.

The game works on many systems and is usually used as an aim trainer as the game requries high presision and aim . It is a game I would highly recommend to players who enjoy music as it is a music based game.


Requirements

  1. Mouse or drawing tablet
  2. keyboard
  3. A decent computer
Osu!standard can also be played on phone
