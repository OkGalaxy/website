What is Catch the Beat?

Ctb or Catch the Beat is a gamemode in osu mania which is unique in the sense that it strays away from the typical rhythm game as you don't exactly tap keys to the beat of a rhythm. You instead move around a chracter that you move to the beat as it catches objects. Its considered one of the easier gamemodes in the cilent.
