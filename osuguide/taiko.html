What is Osu taiko?


Osu!Taiko is a gamemode in the osu cilent that revolves around hitting circles on a drumlike interface. The game is similar to osu mania but differs in alot of playstyle.

How to play

Playing taiko uses 4 keys, two for the inner drum and two for the outer drum.It does not matter which key you press as long as it is on the correct drum either being inner(red) or outer(blue)
